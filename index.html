About Us

We are a fully-fledged software solutions company. We aspire to enable you grow your business to the next level by offering a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform to your enterprise keep timely records of its transactions. While at it, we shall help you as the proprietor to understand the business cycle and consequently make your entity a going concern. The software solutions that we would be offering you would encompass the core business functions/areas: Accounting, Finance, Marketing, and Risk Management. Fundamentally, the Accounting module would help generate the basic financial statements for the entities: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement. The module would also compute the fundamental financial ratios and provide recommendations for ensuring that the entities achieve the required ratios. Moreover, the module would incorporate the break-even-analysis/Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) concept to help the MSMEs proprietors to know when they hit the break-even point and also determine their margin of safety as they seek to attain profitability.  The Finance module would rely on fundamental business data such as Earnings After Tax and Periodic Cash Flows to enable the entities to make working capital management and capital budgeting decisions that would be crucial in analyzing the feasibility and viability of investment decisions to be made toward business improvement. The Marketing module would provide a marketplace that connects proprietors to potential buyers to increase their sales.  Finally, in risk management, Blist would compute a credit rating for MSMEs lending solutions while simultaneously offering business advisory regarding how the MSMEs proprietors would mitigate against risk and optimize their operations for sustainability.
